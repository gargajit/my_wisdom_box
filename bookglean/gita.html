Takeaways from The Gita

The thing to do, knowing that the universe will reward single-minded devotion to a cause, is to choose your cause wisely.

Make sure the cause is unselfish and does not hurt anyone else. Make sure that you are not working for it because you want something out of it, or because you want to show up to someone else, but simply because it is right, because it needs to be done.

Krishna:
If you perform your action simply because it needs to be done, without desire for reward, without fear of consequence, if you offer your action to me as a sacrifice, if you are free of jealousy when you act, if you remain the same whether your action brings you success or failure,
that is right action, whatever that action may be.
Faith + Self-discipline (Sacrifice + Hard Work) = Wisdom (leading to) -> Happiness (NO doubts + No fear).

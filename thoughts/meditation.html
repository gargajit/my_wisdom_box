Meditation

Meditation is a tool that will give the grace and mindfulness to ease through life.

Meditation is your personal journey, an intimate one.

Meditation is your ability to retain your virtues in the face of all adversities.

Levels or Milestones of Meditation:

  • Meditation as an act: training your mind requires discipline and determination.
  • Meditation becomes your second nature: Here effortlessness arises.
  • Meditation becomes a state of your mind: You no longer do meditation, you are in meditation.
  • Liberation.

Meditation is the pathway to your highest self, transforming challenges into growth and effort into ease. As you journey through its stages, meditation evolves from a conscious act into an inherent state of being.

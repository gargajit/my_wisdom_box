Improve on how to prompt

As the Artificial Intelligence-Large Language Model (AI-LLM) applications becoming mainstream, knowing how to prompt is a skill you cannot stay away. The power of writing good prompt can speed up all the help and interactions with such applications.

ChatGPT, CoPilot etc. are now now well established applications among Students, Professionals like Developers, Content Creators, Marketers and Business Analysts are heavy users of such LLM applications.
Hence, knowing how to prompt, is a good skill to have. Let's dive into it.

Tips to help acing the prompt-crafting skills

To get better at crafting prompts that lead to accurate and insightful answers, you should:

1. Be Specific and Clear

  • Identify the Key elements: Focus on the main concept or idea you want to convey. Think about the specific details that define what you're asking.
  • Use Descriptive Language: Include adjectives or phrases that describe the context, especially if you're looking for a precise term.

    • Example:

    • Less specific: "What do you call it when people make up something about an event?"
    • More specific: "What's the term for when people believe in secret plots related to famous events that might not be true?"

2. Provide Context

  • Explain the Situation: If there's a particular scenario or context you're thinking of, include it in your prompt.
  • Use Examples: Briefly mention a related example if it helps clarify what you're asking.

    • Example:

    • "What do you call it when people think that a major historical event was secretly orchestrated by a hidden group?"

3. Be Concise but Complete

  • Avoid Overloading Details: While context is important, keep your prompt focused. Avoid unnecessary details that might confuse the question.
  • Cover All Bases: Make sure you include all relevant aspects that might affect the response.

    • Example:

    • "What's the term for an unproven belief that a secret group is behind a well-known event?"

These are the 4 simple yet effective ideas you should always keep in the back of the mind to get the best and effective results from applications like ChatGPT.

Conclusion

The more you practice crafting prompts, the better you'll get. After receiving a response, reflect on how you could have asked differently to get an even more precise answer. Practice regularly, and over time, it'll become second nature! Keep Prompting :)

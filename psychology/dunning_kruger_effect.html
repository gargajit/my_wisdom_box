Unskilled and Unaware of it

How difficulties in recognizing one's own incompotence lead to Inflated self-assessments.

When you first start learning, you feel like you know so much.
But then later as you gain knowledge, you realise there's a lot more stuff out there that you don't know.

Then you fall into a pit, which is called the Valley of Despair, sometimes referred to as Imposter Syndrome, where even though you feel like you've spent a lot of time learing the skill, you feel like an imposter because you think people are going to find out that "I actually don't know anything."

Both of these are exaggerations.

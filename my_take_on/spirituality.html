My take on: Spirituality

Religion and SPirituality are the two hands, they work together but they are different.

For many religious activity is also their spiritual way. For others, both are separate and they prefer to keep it separate. Hence, even an atheist might be in a spiritual journey.

What is Spirituality? Spirituality is a personal journey towards something larger than life. Spirituality is a very broad concept that relates to so many things like search of meaning, purpose, and connection in life or to something more, a relation with the higher power.
Therefore, it is closely knitted with religion and can involve with practices, beliefs associated with religion.
For many others, it transcend beyond religion and is related to personal growth, inner peace, figuring out their or place in the world.

Spirituality can manifest in various ways, which include: meditation, contemplation, involvement with the society or humanity in general, or a deep appreciation for the natural world.

The meaning itself can be evolving in one's lifetime.

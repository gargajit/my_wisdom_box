My take on: Religion

"Religion plays a key role in every person's life."
It's upto the person how to use religion in his/her daily discourse.

Religion has an affect on individual and the masses. Hence it becomes extra special and sincere how we should deal with the topic of religion.

It has the power to make a person experience the ecstasy of life and/or make another so bitter that drives them into heinous crimes.

Religion has the power, arguably science (yet) may not have. It directs the entire discourse of humanity. Therefore, the topic should be discussed with extreme caution and with mindfulness.

Religion is mostly noble, and it talks about collective good. It acts as a guide, a user manual or sometimes a light house on how to carry life that might have a bigger purpose than one's day to day struggles. The idea is to make the life of a religious person simple when he/she is troubled. The bottom line is, how one takes the religion into his/her life. That makes a whole lot of difference and creates various perceptions about the same concept of religion.

It would not be wrong to say that the power religion holds if misunderstood can have a very serious impact to the humanity. Therefore, reiterating the point that the topic of religion must be dealt in utmost honesty and by all means be avoided as a tool for personal or political gains.

The importance of religion has been in past, is and will always be there in the forseeable future.
